Why We Exist

Tacoma is a city of incredible people, but sometimes the help you need is hidden behind red tape or disconnected systems. South Sound Resource Network was born from a simple idea: No one should have to navigate life's challenges alone. We coordinate the experts so you can focus on your future.

Our Resource Pillars

Providing verified access to vital services across the region.

Housing Stability

Connecting families with regional housing program support and emergency coordination.

Program Support

Assisting local nonprofits with administrative and operational logistics to increase their reach.

Advocacy

Giving a voice to South Sound residents by bridging the gap between people and policymakers.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about how the Network operates.

Is the South Sound Resource Network a government agency?

No. We are an independent resource coordination network that partners with both government and private community organizations to streamline aid.

How do I verify my volunteer hours?

Volunteers like Morgan and Jordan track their hours through our internal portal. Branden Miller provides official verification for school or court requirements upon request.

Where is your main office located?

We operate as a mobile network across the South Sound (Tacoma, Puyallup, and Lakewood) to meet our partners where they are.
