A Message from the Founder

At the South Sound Resource Network, we believe that a stronger Tacoma starts with better coordination. I founded this network to bridge the gap between community needs and the programs designed to meet them. Whether we are providing general program support or coordinating vital local resources, our goal is to ensure that no resident of the South Sound is left without a path forward.
