Why We Exist

Tacoma is a city of incredible people, but sometimes the help you need is hidden behind red tape or disconnected systems. South Sound Resource Network was born from a simple idea: No one should have to navigate life's challenges alone. We coordinate the experts so you can focus on your future.

Our Resource Pillars

We are currently mapping the South Sound to provide verified access to these vital services.

Housing Stability

Connecting families with regional housing program support and emergency coordination.

Program Support

Assisting local nonprofits with administrative and operational logistics to increase their reach.

Advocacy

Giving a voice to South Sound residents by bridging the gap between people and policymakers.

The Roadmap Forward

Our commitment to the South Sound is long-term. Here is how we are expanding our reach to better serve our neighbors.

Immediate Focus

Digital Resource Mapping

We are digitizing our local asset maps to provide an interactive directory for Pierce County residents to find help in real-time.

Summer 2024

Community Partner Summit

Gathering regional nonprofits in Downtown Tacoma to synchronize efforts, reduce service overlap, and share vital data.

Fall 2024 & Beyond

Establishing a permanent volunteer advocacy council to represent South Sound interests at the state level.
