Why We Exist

Tacoma is a city of incredible people, but help can be hard to find in disconnected systems. South Sound Resource Network bridges the gap. We coordinate the assets so you can focus on your future.

The Roadmap Forward

Our commitment to the South Sound is active and evolving. Here is what we are achieving this year.

In Progress
Winter 2026

Digital Resource Mapping

We are currently digitizing our local asset maps to provide an interactive, real-time directory for Pierce County residents. Phase 1 deployment covers Tacoma and Lakewood.

Upcoming
Spring 2026

Spring Community Partner Summit

Gathering regional nonprofits in Downtown Tacoma to synchronize 2026 service goals, reduce overlap, and share vital community data.

Planning
Fall 2026

Regional Advocacy Expansion

Establishing a permanent volunteer advocacy council to represent South Sound interests at the state level during the next legislative session.
